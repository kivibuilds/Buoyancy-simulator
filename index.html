🏛️ Archimedes' Principle
"An object immersed in a fluid experiences an upward buoyant force equal to the weight of the fluid displaced."

Fb = ρ_liquid × V_submerged × g

This object displaces 5.0 kg of liquid, generating a buoyant force of 49.1 N!
🧠 Scientific Explanation
The object has a density of 500.0 kg/m³ while the liquid has a density of 1000 kg/m³.

Since object density is less than liquid density, the buoyant force exceeds gravity. The object floats!
